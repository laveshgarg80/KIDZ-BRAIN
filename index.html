About

Do you have Maths phobia ? Do you want to increase your logical and numerical skiils ?
You have landed on the right place . We at Kidz Brain Academy work to nurture the Mental and Arithmetic Abilities of students of Age group 6-18 Years . We offer courses like Vedic Mathematics , Abacus , Logical Reasoning which help students to build their core acumen at maths and help them to increase concentration & retention power , confidence and shine in this competitive world . We provide structured and engaging classes with an aim to remove maths phobia in students. So if you also want to enhance your numerical skills and stand apart from the crowd then book your free demo class .

Abacus

The Abacus helps your children to develop speed and accuracy in solving math problems by using simple methods. An Abacus student can most likely get the correct answer 4 to 5 times faster than a calculator and besides that, you can't just go around carrying a calculator at all times.

Vedic Maths

Vedic math helps children remember formulae and tables in a much simpler manner and it is one of the fastest methods to learn calculation.Mastering Vedic Maths helps kids perform calculations in a faster manner and even solve simple numeric sums of additions, subtractions, divisions, and multiplication.

Reasoning

Mathematical reasoning is important as it helps to develop critical thinking and understand Maths in a more meaningful way. The concepts of reasoning not only helps the students to have a deeper understanding of the subject but also helps in having a wider perspective to logical statements.
